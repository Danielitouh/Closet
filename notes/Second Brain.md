# Second Brain

A second brain is an external, searchable, *linked* store of what you learn. The point is not storage — it is [[Atomic Notes]] connected by [[Wikilinks]] so ideas resurface when they are relevant.

Core practices: [[Zettelkasten]], [[Evergreen Notes]], [[Progressive Summarization]], and a regular [[Weekly Review]].

This wiki follows [[Karpathy Second Brain Idea]]: let an AI agent do the reading and filing, while you do the thinking.
