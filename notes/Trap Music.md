# Trap Music

The dominant hip hop sound of the 2010s onward, born in Atlanta ([[Southern Hip Hop]]): booming 808 sub-bass, rapid-fire rolling hi-hats, dark synthesized melodies — synthesis where [[Boom Bap]] used dusty samples, though modern producers like Metro Boomin blend both traditions ([[Sampling in Hip Hop]]).

Trap and its internet-native sibling SoundCloud rap surged in the mid-to-late 2010s, powering the "blog era → streaming era" shift. In 2017 that wave carried [[Hip Hop]] past rock to become the best-selling genre in the United States — 44 years after a Bronx rec-room party ([[Birth of Hip Hop]]).
