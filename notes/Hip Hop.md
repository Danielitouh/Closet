# Hip Hop

A culture and art movement born at a Bronx house party in 1973 ([[Birth of Hip Hop]]), built on [[The Four Elements of Hip Hop]]: DJing, MCing, breaking, and graffiti. What began as a way to keep a dance floor moving became, by 2017, the best-selling music genre in the United States.

The musical throughline: [[DJ Kool Herc]] isolating the drum break ([[Breakbeat]]) → looping and chopping records ([[Sampling in Hip Hop]]) → distinct production eras and sounds ([[Boom Bap]], [[Trap Music]]) → regional scenes competing and cross-pollinating ([[East Coast vs West Coast]], [[Southern Hip Hop]]).

Its critical and creative peak is often dated to the [[Golden Age of Hip Hop]] (mid-1980s to mid-1990s).
