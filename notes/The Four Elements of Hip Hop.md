# The Four Elements of Hip Hop

[[Hip Hop]] is a culture, not just a music genre. Its four pillars:

1. **DJing** — turntables and mixing as an instrument ([[DJ Kool Herc]], [[Breakbeat]])
2. **MCing (rapping)** — rhythmic rhyming over beats; grew from party shout-outs (Coke La Rock at the [[Birth of Hip Hop]]) with roots in African oral tradition and Jamaican toasting
3. **Breaking (b-boying/b-girling)** — the acrobatic dance born in the breaks; an Olympic event since 2024
4. **Graffiti (writing)** — the visual element

Many add a fifth element: **knowledge of self/consciousness** — the idea (championed by Afrika Bambaataa's Zulu Nation) that the culture carries an ethic, not just an aesthetic. It surfaces musically in the conscious strain of the [[Golden Age of Hip Hop]].
