# Wikilinks

Double-bracket links like `[[Note Title]]` (with `[[Target|alias]]` support). They are the connective tissue of the wiki: cheap to write, and every one adds an edge to [[Graph View]] and a backlink to the target.

Linking to a note that does not exist yet creates a *ghost* — a faded node that invites creation. See [[Atomic Notes]].
