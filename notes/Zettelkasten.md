# Zettelkasten

Niklas Luhmann's slip-box method: small [[Atomic Notes]], each with a unique address, densely linked. The magic is in the links — a note's neighbors give it meaning.

Modern digital versions relax the numbering and lean on [[Wikilinks]] and [[Graph View]] instead. Related: [[Evergreen Notes]], [[Digital Garden]].
